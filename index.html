The Startup

For this project, we chose to design a mobile interface for Slingshot, a YC startup that automatically monitors and reduces people's medical bills.

Their product works as follows: when a patient gets a medical bill they upload it to Slingshot, and Slingshot analyzes the bills to find the errors, the up-codes, the items insurance should have covered but didnâ€™t, and the items that can be negotiated to a lower cost. Slingshot handles all the time-consuming interactions with hospitals and insurance companies to fix errors and negotiate costs.
